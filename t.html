Frequently Asked Questions

What documents are required for a gold loan?

expand_more

We require minimal documentation. A valid government-issued ID proof (Aadhar Card, Voter ID, etc.) and a passport-sized photograph are typically sufficient.

Is my gold safe with you?

expand_more

Absolutely. Your gold is stored in high-security vaults at our Head Office, monitored 24/7 by surveillance systems and fully insured against theft or damage.

How quickly can I get the loan?

expand_more

Our process is designed for speed. Once your gold is valued and documents verified, the loan amount is typically transferred to your account instantly.

Can I release my gold partially?

expand_more

Yes, we offer partial release options. You can pay a portion of the principal amount and release a proportionate amount of your pledged gold.
